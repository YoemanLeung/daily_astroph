#### [Evidence for higher black hole spin in radio-loud quasars](https://arxiv.org/abs/1710.01440)

#### [WISDOM Project - III: Molecular gas measurement of the supermassive black hole mass in the barred lenticular galaxy NGC442](https://arxiv.org/abs/1710.01488)
    * 用ALMA 12CO(3-2)定一个有bar和boxy bulge的ETG的中心SMBH质量，中心有一个nuclear分子气体disk
    * Disk有inner truncation，可能来自气体不稳定性和tidal shear


#### [Properties of the molecular gas in the fast outflow in the Seyfert galaxy IC 5063](https://arxiv.org/abs/1710.01570)
    * Jet旁边就有快速的分子气体外流; 光学薄，质量比电离气体多
    * 但外流的质量速率和ISM总量比有限，对星系整体影响有限
    * the outflow in IC 5063 is driven by the radio plasma jet expanding into a clumpy gaseous medium and creating a cocoon of (shocked) gas which is pushed away from the jet axis resulting in a lateral outflow, very similar to what is predicted by numerical simulations.

#### [The origin of the X-ray, radio and HI structures in the NGC 5903 galaxy group](https://arxiv.org/abs/1710.01650)

    * 可能来自相互作用导致的AGN outburst，或者是星系和HI filament的高速碰撞

#### [Evolution of Molecular Clouds in the Superwind Galaxy NGC 1808 Probed by ALMA Observations](https://arxiv.org/abs/1710.01829)
    * The distribution and CO luminosities of molecular clouds in the central 1 kpc starburst region indicate an evolutionary sequence, from gas accretion onto the 500 pc ring from the large-scale bar, to enhanced star formation in the ring, and outflow as feedback.

#### [The Spectral Energy Distribution of Powerful Starburst Galaxies I: Modelling the Radio Continuum](https://arxiv.org/abs/1710.01967)
    * 近邻星暴星系的Radio SED往往不是single power law的，有低频的turnover，可能来自free-free的吸收；free-free在高频dominate over synchrotron.
    * Free-free成分和同步加速辐射均和FIR相关
        - 同步加速辐射主导了1.4GHz的射电辐射，但是是delayed SFH tracer; free-free直接来自HII区的电离，是瞬时的SF tracer
